About Us

Palashbari T. R. Sub Division under PWD (Roads), Mirza is responsible for the construction, maintenance, and development of road infrastructure in the Palashbari region. We ensure safe and sustainable transport for the people of Assam.

Services

  • Road construction & maintenance
  • Rural connectivity development
  • Bridge and culvert supervision
  • Infrastructure inspection and reports
  • Public grievance redressal
